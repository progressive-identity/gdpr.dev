GDRP.dev offers you tools to generate machine-readable GDPR compliant agreements. With this, you can create easily compliant permissions and authorization windows according to all the necessary 13 GDPR requirements as finality, jurisdiction, destination, duration, profiling etc....

GDPR JSON Web Token

GDPR.dev gives you the specifications to generate GDRP JWT as tokens that includes as payload a machine-readable compliant GDPR agreement. As the contract is serialized in the token, developers can stay compliant programmatically and manage easily users data regulations requests.

Go to the Project on Github

GDPR OAuth 2.0 server

GDPR.dev gives you the reference design and the code of a decentralized OAuth2.0 server, that handles GDPR JWT. As the token are stateless, the GDPR OAuth server is able to get data from any resource server as any HTTP endpoint and grant access to any client application in a total decentralized way

Frequently asked questions

About Personal data regulation in USA and Europe

What allow regulations for data of my users?

Reguations in USA and Europe enable you to get your data back in a machine readable format and transfer them to other platforms to obtain better services and access data-enabled features. In Europe, this is with the  the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) article 20.  In USA, it is with the the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) Section 1798.100(d)

What are my user's rights?

With these regulations, your users can access, export, rectify or erase their personal data you have on them.

What can my users do with this data?

You can store the data in a place you control for your privacy, you can ask to modify or erase your date and most importantly you can export them to another service

How long does it take?

Data requests usually take a few hours to few days, but by law companies have a maximum of 30 days to give you back your data

How much does it cost?

Requesting your data to platforms is free by default.
